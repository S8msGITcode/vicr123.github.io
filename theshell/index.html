For the busy

When you're not actively using theShell, the bar shifts into the background so you won't be distracted by it. And if you really need to work distraction free, theShell 7.0 comes with the all new Quiet Mode, which allows you to control, on-the-fly, the notifications and sounds that your computer will emit.

The gateway to your computer

theShell comes with the powerful Gateway, which is a central place to act with your computer. And like everything else, it doesn't appear unless you're focusing on it.

A contemporary look

theShell uses the in-house Contemporary theme by default, which gives theShell a contemporary and modern look, and all your apps will look pleasant and will integrate with features of theShell.
