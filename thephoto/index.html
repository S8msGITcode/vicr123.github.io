Everyone's the cameraman

With Event Mode, everyone with an Android™ device can use thePhoto and send photos to the big screen!

For Everyone

thePhoto is available on Windows, macOS and Linux. It's also available for Android. How's that for operating system support?

Collates your images

When using Event Mode, all the photos taken during a session are saved on the host's computer. That way, pictures don't need to be sent between everyone; they're all collated on the host's computer on the fly.
