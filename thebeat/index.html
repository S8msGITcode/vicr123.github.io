theBeat

Keeps your computer beating.

And theBeat 2.0 is waiting for your beats! Go get it!

See the code

For the visual listeners

theBeat comes with 3 visualisations, and there are more coming soon.

For the organised

theBeat allows you to easily manage your playlists and music library so your music is always one click away.

For the workers

theBeat supports the Media Player Remote Interfacing Specification, which means that it will integrate into your desktop environment of choice, and media can be controlled without even touching theBeat. And on theShell, your music can be controlled from the lock screen.
